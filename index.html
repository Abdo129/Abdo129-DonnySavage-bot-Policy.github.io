DonnySavage Bot Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we handle your information.

2025-6-9

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how DonnySavage ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information when you interact with it on Discord.

2. Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary for moderation:

  • Server IDs: For configuration storage
  • User IDs: For moderation tracking
  • Message Metadata: Author, timestamp, channel
  • Attachments: Temporary storage

We never keep data more than 24 hours to respect Discord's terms of service

3. How We Use Data

Data is used exclusively for:

  • Effective Moderation and safety enforcement
  • Bot functionality (scheduled messages, welcome systems, etc.)
  • Debugging and improvement

4. Data Retention

  • Message data: 24 hours maximum
  • Attachments: 24 hours maximum
  • Server configurations: Until bot removal

5. Your Rights

You can exercise your rights with these commands:

  • View your data: /request-my-data
  • Delete your data: /delete-my-data
  • Adjust preferences: /privacy
